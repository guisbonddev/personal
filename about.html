About Me


My name is Will Guisbond. I'm currently a senior at Jamesville-Dewitt High School and have lived in Syracuse my entire life. I proudly consider myself a creator as my roots started in web development and coding, after teaching myself HTML at the age of 13. Fast forward a few years, I'm an Operations Intern at Hack Upstate, an organization that helps to uplift the Central / Upper New York technology communities by hositng bi-annual hackathons and coding bootcamps.

I've always loved creating, whether that's by coding a website, organizing a community event, or building something with my hands. As a result, I'm a flexible technologist, and someone who loves to tackle complex problems, no matter what they are.

Outside of school and work, I'm an avid tennis player and am an FAA licensed Private Pilot. I've always enjoyed being outdoors and have a knack for music, whether it's simply listening to it or playing on my trumpet. These hobbies, and the adventures I often find myself on as a result of them, often inspire my work -- it's something I find great passion in.

In the fall of 2020, I'll be attending the University of Vermont to study Mechanical Engineering in their Honors College.
